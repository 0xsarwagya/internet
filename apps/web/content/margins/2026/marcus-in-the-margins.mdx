---
title: Marcus, in the margins
date: 2026-07-05
category: books
---

Marcus Aurelius did not write for you. He wrote for the emperor he was trying to become the day after tomorrow.

The margin notes I keep, on any book, are the same. They are not for the future reader. They are for the reader I have not become yet, sitting with the same page, wondering why he underlined this one.
