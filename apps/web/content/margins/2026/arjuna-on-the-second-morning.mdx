---
title: Arjuna, on the second morning
date: 2026-07-28
category: myth
---

On the first morning at Kurukshetra, Arjuna asked why he should fight. The book he got in reply is long enough that most readers stop before the answer feels earned.

There is a second morning, though, that nobody writes about. He gets up. He puts on the armour. He does not ask again.

That, I think, is the part I have been avoiding.
