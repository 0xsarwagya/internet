## Pinned vectors

The v1 test vectors are byte-pinned for every deterministic surface:
canonicalization, commitments, tree structure, header leaves, receipt
serialization. Signatures are pinned in the verify direction only —
Ed25519 signatures may not be byte-identical across engines (WebKit
hedges as side-channel hardening), but the pinned signature must verify
everywhere.

Any implementation, in any language, that claims specification 1.0
conformance must recompute these vectors.
